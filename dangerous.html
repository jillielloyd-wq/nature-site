⚠️ Lethal Wildlife

Respect is the first rule of the wilderness. Knowing the dangers is vital.

The Lethal List (Expanded):

  1. Inland Taipan (Snake): Most toxic venom of any snake. One bite could kill 100 men.
  2. Sydney Funnel-Web Spider: One of the few spiders that can prove fatal to humans.
  3. Grizzly Bear: Not aggressive, but extremely protective. Attacks are powerful.
  4. Box Jellyfish: The 'invisible killer' of the ocean. Stings are instant agony and cardiotoxic.

Can you survive a bear encounter?

  • The Black Mamba is actually olive-grey, but its mouth is ink-black.
  • The Inland Taipan is found in a tiny, specific part of Australia.
  • Rattlesnake venom is usually hemotoxic (attacks tissue) but can sometimes be neurotoxic.
