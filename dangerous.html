⚠️ Lethal Wildlife

Respect the Wild:

The Inland Taipan: The most venomous snake on Earth. One bite contains enough toxin to kill 100 men.

The Box Jellyfish: Their stings are incredibly painful and can be fatal to humans in minutes.

The Grizzly Bear: Powerful and protective. Always keep a safe distance in bear country.
