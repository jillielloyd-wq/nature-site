Preserve the Wild

Understanding Global Warming

Our climate is changing due to excess carbon being released into the atmosphere. The impact on ecosystems includes:

  • Habitat loss (polar bears losing ice)
  • Coral bleaching (ocean acidification)
  • Increased wildfires (forest stress)

Preventative Actions:

Small actions lead to big changes. Here is how you can help protect our wild spaces:

  • Reduce Energy: Turn off lights, use efficient appliances.
  • Support Clean Energy: Vote and push for solar/wind power.
  • Leave No Trace: Never litter in the forest; pack out what you pack in.
  • Protect Wetlands: Wetlands filter carbon better than rainforests.
