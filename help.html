Preserving Our Future

Local Clean-Ups

Picking up litter protects soil health and local water sources. Small efforts like park clean-ups prevent wildlife from getting injured by plastic waste.

Protecting Our Oceans

Oceans are the lungs of the planet. Reducing single-use plastic and supporting large-scale ocean clearing projects helps restore coral reefs and marine life.
