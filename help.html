How to Help Preserve Nature

Small actions lead to big changes. Here is how you can help:

  • Reduce Waste: Use reusable bags and bottles.
  • Stay on Trails: Protect fragile plants and habitats.
  • Local Flora: Plant native flowers to help local bees and birds.
  • Pick up Trash: Even if it isn't yours, it helps the forest.
