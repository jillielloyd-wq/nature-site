How to Preserve Our World 🌎

Get Involved! 🌎

🌟 **Reduce, Reuse, Recycle:** Cut down on plastic use to keep our oceans clean.

🌳 **Plant a Tree:** Help provide oxygen and homes for birds and squirrels.

🚶 **Walk More:** Reduce pollution by walking or biking instead of driving.

💧 **Save Water:** Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth!
