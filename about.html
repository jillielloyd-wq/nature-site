Understanding Our Planet

Nature is a complex system of living and non-living things working together. Here is how ecosystems function:

Core Ecosystems:

  • Rainforests: Host over half of the world's wildlife. 🌳
  • Oceans: Produce over 50% of the Earth's oxygen (thanks, plankton!) 🌊
  • Deserts: Crucial indicators of climate health. 🌵
  • Wetlands: The 'Earth's kidneys' that filter water. 🐸

Did You Know? (Nature Facts)

  • There are more trees on Earth than stars in the Milky Way (about 3 trillion!).
  • A single oak tree can support over 500 species of animals and fungi.
  • The "wood wide web" allows trees to talk and share resources through their roots.
