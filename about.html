Understand Our Planet

Nature is an intricate web where every organism, from the smallest bacteria to the blue whale, plays a specific role. Ecosystems provide services that humans cannot live without, such as clean water and oxygen.

Biodiversity makes an ecosystem more resilient. When we protect one species, we often protect an entire habitat that supports hundreds of others.
