The Trophic Cascade

Ecosystems are balanced systems. When we protect one species, we often protect an entire habitat that supports hundreds of others. One fascinating concept is the "Trophic Cascade"—where removing an apex predator, like a tiger or wolf, affects the entire food chain, altering vegetation and even river patterns.

If you click on the forest or tiger photos, you’ll see a rain of **🌱, 🌿, 🌴, 🌳** emojis. These symbolize the vibrant growth that a healthy, interconnected ecosystem supports. Protecting biodiversity is key to ensuring that this web remains strong for future generations!
