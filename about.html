The Interconnected Web

Nature is a balanced system where every organism, from the smallest bacteria to the blue whale, plays a specific role. Forests provide services that humans cannot live without, such as clean water and oxygen.

If you click on the forest or tiger photos, you’ll see a faint rain of **🌱, 🌿, 🌴, 🌳** emojis. These symbolize the vibrant growth that a healthy, interconnected ecosystem supports. Protecting biodiversity is key to ensuring that this web remains strong for future generations!
