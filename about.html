Understand Our Planet

Nature is a complex system of living and non-living things working together. From the rainforests to the deep oceans, every ecosystem plays a vital role in our survival.

  • Rainforests produce the oxygen we breathe.
  • Oceans regulate our global climate.
  • Bees and pollinators ensure our food supply.
