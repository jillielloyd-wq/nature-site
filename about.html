Understand Our Planet

Nature is a complex system of living and non-living things working together. From the smallest fungi to the largest whales, everything is connected.

Fascinating Ecosystems:

  • Rainforests: Known as the "lungs of the planet," they produce a huge amount of the world's oxygen.
  • The Oceans: Covering over 70% of Earth, they are home to millions of undiscovered species.
  • The Underground: Trees actually communicate with each other through a network of fungi in the soil!
